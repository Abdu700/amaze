ARDAH MUSEUM
LOGO
HERE
It was our delight to collaborate with Sadu in participating in the Saudi Ardah Exhibition held in Safat Square for an extended period of six days. The exhibition aims to offer a sensory experience that harmonizes the past and the present, incorporating historical content within a visual framework inspired by desert nature.

Notably, the exhibition features a visual presentation of the horseshoe show, reminiscent of King Abdulaziz's performance alongside knights. This exhibition serves as a testament to the Ministry of Culture's ongoing commitment to introducing the ancient history of the Saudi Ardah and elevating the significance of Saudi identity and its enduring value for future generations.
